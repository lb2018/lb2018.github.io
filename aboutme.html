About Me

I am a higher education teacher, adult educator, and researcher originally from New Zealand. I have been studying, teaching and working in public health since 2009.

I completed my Master of Public Health at the University of Melbourne in Australia in 2012. After graduating I became a tutor on the programme. I was then appointed as a sessional lecturer in health promotion at the Australian Catholic University. In 2015 I took up a permanent position as lecturer in health promotion at Unitec Institute of Technology in Auckland, New Zealand, and was promoted to senior lecturer in 2018.

My areas of interest are sexual health, global health, health promotion, health education, health programmes, programme evaluation, adult education, and higher education teaching.
